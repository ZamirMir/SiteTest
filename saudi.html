Mohamed AbdulAziz Alaskar Market

Al-Anduls Branch

The Mohamed Alaskar Market was established in 1400 H (1980 AD) from the city of Al-Kharj, Saudi to provide services in the selling and distribution sector for food and consumer goods for the Saudi market with the latest methods and health specifications.
Mohammed Alaskar Market sells and supplies all kinds of consumer goods, whether through distribution channels for retail stores or wholesale distribution channels for food services such as restaurants, cafes, catering and subsistence companies. The Foundation is keen to distribute the finest local and imported goods.
