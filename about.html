The Group History

Al-Kharj, KSA

Our Company

The Mohamed Abdulalaziz alaskar Trading Co. was established in 1400 H (1980 AD) from the city of Al-Kharj, Saudi to provide services in the selling and distribution sector for food and consumer goods, logictic Services and Real Estate for the Saudi market with the latest methods. Mohammed Alaskar Market has vast experience in the field of trade and food distribution which exceeds 40 years of experience in this domain. Our market distributes food for retail price through its markets which are spread throughout the city in addition to the wholesale distribution and supply prices for catering companies and the supply of food products for small storages and fast food restaurants, commercial centers and others, the organization is keen to distribute the resources according to the requirements of food safety and health in accordance with the instructions of the Food and Drug Administration by securing all the conditions of products during storage, transportation and distribution.

Vision & Mission

Our Vision is a National Vision in accordance with Saudi Arabia's vision 2030. It focuses on development and reaches out to the latest and best Services. Strengthening the company's position in the trade of food and consumer sector, according to the mechanisms of action include the following:

  • Looking forward to be one of the best food distributors in Saudi Arabia and to specialize in distributing the best products of local and international companies in terms of quality and appropriate price.
  • Giving more focus for our partners in success like clients, suppliers and employees.
  • Commitment to quality and work according to professional standards
